# Sureplus Market Website

**Link:** [sureplus.net](http://sureplus.net)
**Technology Used:** React, TailwindCSS, antDesign

---

## Overview
Sureplus is a Davao City social enterprise reducing food waste by reselling, repurposing, and reallocating surplus food. Our app connects food businesses with customers seeking affordable food, with unsold food made into products or distributed to the needy.
